Our Volunteers

Life in the oceans is endangered by pollution, overfishing, illegal fishing, habitat destruction, and climate change. Our team of marine scientists, economists, lawyers and other collaborators are achieving specific and concrete changes in legislation to prevent the irreversible collapse of fish stocks, protect marine mammals and other forms of marine life.
